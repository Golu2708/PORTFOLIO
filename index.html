Energy Monitoring

Sub-Station Energy Monitoring

This project, developed for Hitech Elex LLP, focuses on the integration and monitoring of 30 energy meters (MFM and ABT) using the MODBUS-485 communication protocol. Utilizing SpandanSCADA software, the system is designed to acquire data, generate reports, and monitor energy usage across 5 dedicated screens. A total of 43 SCADA screens will be configured to handle visualization, control, and system monitoring. The project includes the integration of 33 meters, each capturing 13 parameters, with data stored in a centralized database for analysis and custom report generation to support operational efficiency and informed decision-making.
