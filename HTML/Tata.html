Project Overview

This project focuses on the comprehensive monitoring and control of press machines through an advanced ANDON display system. The solution provides real-time visibility into manufacturing operations, enabling production teams to quickly identify and address issues, optimize machine performance, and make data-driven decisions.

The system delivers critical manufacturing insights including:

  • Real-time machine status monitoring and visualization
  • Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE) calculation and analysis
  • Downtime tracking with root cause identification
  • Production loss quantification and reporting
  • Performance comparison across shifts and time periods

Leveraging SCADA technology, the solution collects data directly from press machines, processes it through sophisticated algorithms, and presents actionable information on intuitive ANDON displays strategically positioned throughout the facility. The comprehensive alert system ensures immediate notification of critical events through both visual displays and automated messaging channels.

The system's architecture is designed for scalability and reliability, with data securely stored in a SQLite database for efficient retrieval and analysis.

Key Features

ANDON Display System

Real-time visualization of machine status, production metrics, and operational alerts through large-format displays that provide instant visibility to operators, supervisors, and management across the manufacturing floor.

OEE Calculation & Analysis

Automated calculation of Overall Equipment Effectiveness with detailed breakdown of Availability, Performance, and Quality factors, enabling targeted improvement initiatives to maximize production efficiency.

Downtime Tracking

Comprehensive monitoring of machine downtime events with categorization, duration tracking, and root cause analysis, helping identify recurring issues and prioritize maintenance activities.

Production Loss Analysis

Quantification of production losses due to various factors including machine breakdowns, changeovers, material shortages, and quality issues, with financial impact assessment.

Automated Reporting

Shift-based report generation with automatic email distribution to key stakeholders, providing timely insights without manual intervention. Critical events trigger immediate SMS alerts to designated personnel.

Comparison Analytics

Advanced reporting tools that allow comparison of production data across different time periods, shifts, or machine lines, helping identify best practices and improvement opportunities.
