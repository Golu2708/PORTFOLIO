Project Overview

Our team collaborated with CSIR-National Chemical Laboratory to develop an advanced solution for monitoring and controlling a Distillation System utilizing the OPC DA communication protocol. Employing SpandanSCADA Software, we meticulously crafted 63 interface screens for comprehensive data acquisition, report generation, and real-time status monitoring.

The system encompasses critical components including:

  • Distillation columns (DC-01 and DC-02)
  • Reboilers (RE-01 and RE-02)
  • Pumps (P-01 to P-11)
  • Condensers (HE-03 and HE-06)
  • Tanks/vessels (HV-01 to HV-10)

Control loops were intricately implemented to regulate feed stream flow rates, manage reflux ratios, oversee reboiler temperatures, and optimize condenser performance. To ensure operational safety, robust safety interlocks were established, complemented by proactive alarms and alerts for prompt anomaly detection.

Our user-friendly graphical interface (GUI) was meticulously designed for intuitive operation, empowering operators to effortlessly monitor process variables, control equipment, and swiftly respond to alerts.
