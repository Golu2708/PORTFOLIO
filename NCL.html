Our team collaborated with CSIR- National Chemical Laboratory to develop an advanced solution for monitoring and controlling a Distillation System utilizing the OPC DA communication protocol. Employing SpandanSCADA Software, we meticulously crafted 63 interface screens for comprehensive data acquisition, report generation, and real-time status monitoring.

The system encompasses critical components such as distillation columns (DC-01 and DC-02), reboilers (RE-01 and RE-02), pumps (P-01 to P-11), condensers (HE-03 and HE-06), and tanks/vessels (HV-01 to HV-10).

Control loops were intricately implemented to regulate feed stream flow rates, manage reflux ratios, oversee reboiler temperatures, and optimize condenser performance. To ensure operational safety, robust safety interlocks were established, complemented by proactive alarms and alerts for prompt anomaly detection.

Our user-friendly graphical interface (GUI) was meticulously designed for intuitive operation, empowering operators to effortlessly monitor process variables, control equipment, and swiftly respond to alerts.

Seamless bi-directional communication between the SCADA system and the plant's PLC control system facilitated smooth data exchange, ensuring seamless integration with existing control algorithms and protocols.

Additionally, we enabled comprehensive data logging of process parameters for historical analysis and troubleshooting, seamlessly transferring data to a designated database using SpandanSCADA software.

Lastly, our solution includes insightful reports on system performance, equipment utilization, and production rates, providing invaluable data-driven insights for optimization and strategic decision-making.

This integrated solution not only enhances operational efficiency and safety but also empowers informed decision-making in the distillation process, underscoring our proficiency in SCADA system integration and control.
